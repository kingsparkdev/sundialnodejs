Diurnal circle of the Sun - Simulation

The main principle of the Angbuilgu is to project the sun's Diurnal motion onto the Siban. But did you know that this Diurnal circle of the sun is not the same every day? Because the ecliptic and the Earth's equator do not coincide, the Diurnal circle of the sun changes depending on the season. The different intervals between the Jeolgiseons are also due to this principle. The Diurnal circle of the sun also changes depending on the latitude. This is because the angle between the horizon and the Diurnal circle of the sun changes. This is why the position of the north celestial pole changes, and the angle of the Yeongchim must also change.
